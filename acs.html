Amadeus Chamber Symphony

The Process

I was awarded a fantastic opportunity by Amadeus Chamber Symphony when they agreed to bring me on as their Web Master. Amadeus is a 501C3 symphony operating out of St. John's University in Collegevile, MN. Being a performing organization, it was crucial that Amadeus had a website that was updated frequently, included multimedia featuring their performances and valuable organization information including a player roster, season schedule, contact info, and an audition form. There is an Amadeus member login which provides access to a member dashboard. The site is built on WordPress with some custom PHP scripts to power backend data retrieval.

Given the incredible trend mobile traffic is taking, it was absolutely necessary to design the site as responsive and mobile-friendly. The mobile view has a much more streamlined welcome page with important performance information front-and-center. This site was a blast to create! Look out for full-scale deployment in May, 2014.
